# Privacy Policy - Download Gate System

## Legal Documentation for Download Gate Multi-Brand Consent

This document provides the privacy policy language for download gate submissions with multi-brand consent (The Backstage + Gee Beat).

---

## Section to Add to Privacy Policy

### 5. Download Gates and Multi-Brand Consent

#### 5.1 What Are Download Gates?

Download gates are features that allow artists and DJs to share their music with fans in exchange for email consent. When you download content through a download gate, you have the option to consent to receive marketing communications from multiple parties.

#### 5.2 Parties Who May Send You Emails

When you submit your email through a download gate, you may consent to receive emails from:

1. **The Artist/DJ**: The creator who shared the content with you
2. **The Backstage** (thebackstage.app): Our platform that facilitates music distribution
3. **Gee Beat** (gbid.com): Our partner platform for music promotion

#### 5.3 Your Consent Choices

**You Have Control**: When submitting a download gate form, you will see separate checkboxes for each party. You can choose to:

- ✅ Accept emails from The Backstage only
- ✅ Accept emails from Gee Beat only
- ✅ Accept emails from both The Backstage and Gee Beat
- ✅ Accept emails from the Artist/DJ only (required to download)

**Artist/DJ Consent**: Consent to receive emails from the artist/DJ who created the content is always required (since they own the content you're downloading).

**Optional Consents**: Consents to The Backstage and Gee Beat are completely optional. You are NOT required to consent to marketing from these platforms to download the content.

#### 5.4 What Data We Collect

When you submit a download gate form, we collect:

**Required Information**:
- Email address
- Download timestamp
- IP address (for security and GDPR compliance)
- User agent (browser/device information)

**Optional Information**:
- First name (if you choose to provide it)
- Social media verification data (SoundCloud, Spotify, Instagram - if required by the gate)

**Consent Tracking** (GDPR Audit Trail):
- Which brands you consented to (The Backstage, Gee Beat, Artist)
- Timestamp of consent
- IP address at time of consent
- User agent at time of consent
- Download gate slug and track information

#### 5.5 How Your Data Is Used

**By The Artist/DJ**:
- Send you updates about new releases, events, and shows
- Notify you about exclusive content and promotions
- Build their fan community

**By The Backstage**:
- Send marketing emails about platform features
- Notify you about new artists and releases
- Send newsletters about music industry news
- Promotional offers and events

**By Gee Beat**:
- Send marketing emails about music distribution services
- Notify you about partner opportunities
- Send newsletters about music promotion strategies
- Promotional offers and events

#### 5.6 Legal Basis for Processing

**GDPR Article 6(1)(a)**: Consent

- You provide **explicit consent** by checking the consent checkboxes
- Each consent is **separate and specific** to each brand
- You can **withdraw consent** at any time by unsubscribing

**GDPR Article 13**: Right to Information

- We clearly disclose which parties will receive your data
- We explain the purpose of data processing
- We provide links to our full Privacy Policy

#### 5.7 Data Sharing

**Who Receives Your Email**:

When you consent to The Backstage and/or Gee Beat:
- Your email is added to **our mailing list** (The Backstage)
- Your email is added to **Gee Beat's mailing list** (if you consented)
- Your email is added to **the Artist/DJ's contact list** (always)

**Data Sharing Agreement**:
- The Artist/DJ owns their contact list
- The Backstage and Gee Beat maintain separate contact lists
- Each party processes your data independently
- Each party must comply with GDPR and CAN-SPAM

#### 5.8 Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your data:

**Right to Access** (Article 15):
- Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- See which brands you consented to and when

**Right to Rectification** (Article 16):
- Correct inaccurate email address or name

**Right to Erasure** (Article 17):
- Request deletion of your data
- Note: We may anonymize data for legal retention (7 years for GDPR compliance)

**Right to Object** (Article 21):
- Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
- Object to data processing for marketing purposes

**Right to Data Portability** (Article 20):
- Receive your data in machine-readable format (JSON/CSV)

**Right to Withdraw Consent** (Article 7(3)):
- Unsubscribe from The Backstage emails
- Unsubscribe from Gee Beat emails
- Unsubscribe from Artist/DJ emails
- Each unsubscribe is independent (you can unsubscribe from one without affecting others)

#### 5.9 Unsubscribe / Opt-Out

**How to Unsubscribe**:

Every email you receive will include an unsubscribe link in the footer. Clicking this link will:

- Remove you from that specific sender's mailing list
- Log your unsubscribe action (GDPR audit trail)
- Stop future emails from that sender within 10 business days

**Platform-Specific Unsubscribe**:
- The Backstage: [https://thebackstage.app/unsubscribe](https://thebackstage.app/unsubscribe)
- Gee Beat: [https://gbid.com/unsubscribe](https://gbid.com/unsubscribe)
- Artist/DJ: Check the unsubscribe link in their emails

**One-Click Unsubscribe** (CAN-SPAM & Gmail/Outlook):
- All emails include `List-Unsubscribe` headers
- Gmail and Outlook show an unsubscribe button at the top of emails
- One click removes you from the mailing list

#### 5.10 CAN-SPAM Act Compliance

We comply with the CAN-SPAM Act of 2003:

- ✅ We include a valid physical postal address in all emails
- ✅ We include a clear and conspicuous unsubscribe link
- ✅ We honor unsubscribe requests within 10 business days
- ✅ We clearly identify emails as advertisements
- ✅ We don't use deceptive subject lines or headers

#### 5.11 Data Retention

**Active Contacts** (Subscribed):
- We retain your email and consent data as long as you remain subscribed
- We retain consent history for audit trail (GDPR requirement)

**Unsubscribed Contacts**:
- We anonymize your email (e.g., `deleted-123@anonymized.local`)
- We retain anonymized records for 7 years (legal defense, fraud prevention)
- We retain consent history logs for 7 years (GDPR Article 30 requirement)

**Download Tokens**:
- Download tokens expire after 24 hours
- Used tokens are marked as complete and cannot be reused
- Token data retained for 7 years (fraud prevention)

#### 5.12 Security Measures

We protect your data with:

- **Encryption**: TLS/SSL for all data in transit
- **Database Security**: PostgreSQL with row-level security
- **Token Security**: Cryptographically secure random tokens (32 bytes)
- **One-Time Use**: Download tokens can only be used once
- **Expiration**: Tokens expire after 24 hours
- **IP Logging**: Track suspicious activity and prevent abuse

#### 5.13 International Data Transfers

**Data Location**:
- Data is stored in secure data centers (check your hosting provider)
- We use Vercel for hosting (servers in multiple regions)
- We use PostgreSQL databases (specify region if known)

**GDPR Compliance for Transfers**:
- We use Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for international transfers
- We ensure adequate data protection safeguards
- Data transfer mechanisms comply with GDPR Chapter V

#### 5.14 Contact Information

**Data Controller**:
- The Backstage (specify legal entity name)
- Email: privacy@thebackstage.app (or your DPO email)
- Address: (your physical address)

**Data Protection Officer (DPO)**:
- Email: dpo@thebackstage.app (if applicable)

**To Exercise Your Rights**:
- Email: privacy@thebackstage.app
- Subject line: "GDPR Data Request - [Your Email]"
- We will respond within 30 days (GDPR requirement)

#### 5.15 Supervisory Authority

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority:

**EU Citizens**:
- Your local Data Protection Authority
- European Data Protection Board: [https://edpb.europa.eu](https://edpb.europa.eu)

**Spanish Citizens** (if applicable):
- Agencia Española de Protección de Datos (AEPD)
- Website: [https://www.aepd.es](https://www.aepd.es)
